About Me

Designing with purpose & precision

Hi, I'm Nikita — a UI/UX Designer based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat with a Master's in Communication Design from DA-IICT. I create digital products that are beautiful, intuitive, and genuinely solve problems for real people.

Over the past 3+ years, I've designed for restaurants, SaaS platforms, booking systems, cab apps, and international brands — from research and wireframes to polished, production-ready interfaces.

I believe great design isn't decoration — it's strategy you can see. I work at the intersection of usability, aesthetics, and business goals.

My Approach

How I design

Every pixel has a reason. My process ensures design decisions are grounded in empathy, validated by data, and refined through iteration.

01
🔍

Research

Understanding users, their goals, pain points, and the competitive landscape. Every project begins with empathy and curiosity.

→
02
✏️

Wireframe

Rapid sketches and low-fidelity wireframes to explore layout, hierarchy, and user flows before committing to visuals.

→
03
🎨

Design

High-fidelity UI with a strong visual system — typography, color, spacing, and components that feel cohesive and premium.

→
04
🧪

Test & Iterate

Usability testing, feedback loops, and refinement. Great design is never done — it evolves with users and context.
